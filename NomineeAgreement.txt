CUSTODY AND NOMINEE AGREEMENT

This Custody and Nominee Agreement (the Agreement) is made on [DATE] (the Effective Date) between:

(1) Regen Foundation, a Delaware corporation (the Foundation); and 
(2) [NOMINEE], a [●], (the “Nominee”) 
each a Party and together the Parties.

WHEREAS, the Foundation has appointed certain nominees to maintain custody of the Foundation’s Digital Assets, as defined below;

WHEREAS, each nominee is a director or other fiduciary of the Foundation;

WHEREAS, each nominee has a separate and distinct key that is part of a consortium of keys that are all required to gain access to the cold storage wallet that contains the Foundation’s Digital Assets; and

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to enter into this Agreement to memorialize the rights and obligations of each Party with respect to the custody and usage of the Foundation’s Digital Assets;

NOW THEREFORE, IT IS AGREED as follows:

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION 
1.1 In this Agreement, the following words and expressions shall have the following meanings unless the context otherwise requires: 
Assets Tax Documentation has the meaning set forth in Clause 4.2.

Assets means Eligible Assets that have been transferred and / or Delivered as the case may be to the Nominee to be held in accordance with this Agreement.
Authorized Person means the Person(s) listed on the schedule provided to the Nominee by the Foundation simultaneously with the execution of this Agreement, or soon thereafter.

Confidential Information has the meaning given in Clause 6.1.

Delivery (or Deliver or Delivered) means the valid and lawful transfer of Eligible Assets to one or more designated blockchain addresses controlled and provided by the receiving Party to the sending Party for such transfer. Eligible Assets shall be considered Delivered to the Nominee after the prevailing number of network confirmations as required by the Nominee from time to time have occurred on the blockchain used for the transaction transferring the Eligible Assets. 

Digital Asset shall mean a digital asset (also called a “cryptocurrency,” “virtual currency,” “digital currency,” or “digital commodity”), such as RND tokens or bitcoin, which is based on the cryptographic protocol of a computer network that may be (i) centralized or decentralized, (ii) closed or open-source, and (iii) used as a medium of exchange and/or store of value.

Eligible Assets mean Digital Assets that are supported by the Nominee in its sole discretion on any given date, but shall at all times include RND Tokens.

Governmental Authority means any governmental body at the supranational, national, state, county, province, city, municipal, local or any other level, any agency, authority, instrumentality, regulatory body, quasi-regulatory authority, administrative tribunal, central bank, public office, court, arbitration or mediation panel, or other entity or subdivision exercising executive, legislative, judicial, taxing, regulatory or administrative powers or functions of government, securities exchange or self-regulatory organization, in each case in any jurisdiction.

Instructions mean communications received by the Nominee through an on-line communication system, by e-mail, or other method or system, as specified by the Nominee from time to time as available for use in connection with the Services. 

Law means each of the following, including any updates thereto throughout the Term, to the extent applicable: any and all supranational, national, state, provincial or local laws, treaties, rules, regulations, regulatory guidance, directives, policies, orders or determinations of (or agreements with), and mandatory written direction from (or agreements with), any Governmental Authority or other regulatory authority, including export laws, Sanctions regulations, and all federal and state statutes or regulations relating to banking, stored value, money transmission, unclaimed property, payment processing, telecommunications, unfair or deceptive trade practices or acts, anti-corruption, trade compliance, anti-money laundering, terrorist financing, “know your customer,” securities, commodities, derivatives, or privacy or data security.

Person means any natural person, corporation, general partnership, limited partnership, limited liability company, joint venture, trust, proprietorship, governmental body or other entity, association or organization of any nature. Any reference herein to any Person shall be construed to include such Person’s successors and assigns.

Proper Instructions means an Instruction delivered by an Authorized Person (or Person that the Nominee believes in good faith to be an Authorized Person) that is confirmed by an Instruction from at least one additional Authorized Person (or Person that the Nominee believes in good faith to be an Authorized Person).

Services have the meaning given in Clause 2.1. 

Taxes includes all federal, state, local, foreign, and other taxes, government fees or the like, including, without limitation, income taxes, estimated taxes, alternative minimum taxes, franchise taxes, capital stock taxes, sales taxes, use taxes, ad valorem, or value-added taxes, employment and payroll-related taxes, withholding taxes, and transfer taxes, whether or not measured in whole or in part by net income, and all deficiencies, or other additions to tax, interest thereon, and fines and penalties imposed in connection therewith.

1.2 In this Agreement: 
(a) the headings are inserted for convenience only and shall not affect the interpretation of this Agreement; 
(b) references to clauses and schedules are to clauses of, and schedules to, this Agreement; 
(c) references to this Agreement or any other document are to that document as from time to time amended, restated, novated or replaced (however fundamentally); 
(d) references to a person include an individual, firm, company, corporation, unincorporated body of persons and any government entity; 
(e) references to a person include its successors, permitted assignees and permitted transferees; and 
(f) unless the context otherwise requires, wording in the singular shall include the plural and vice versa and words importing gender shall include every gender. 
2. APPOINTMENT OF NOMINEE  
2.1 As of the Effective Date, the Foundation appoints the Nominee to perform the following services detailed in this Clause 2.1 (the Services) in accordance with the terms of this Agreement, and the Nominee hereby accepts such appointment. The Services include the receipt, safekeeping and maintenance of Eligible Assets and associated record-keeping in relation to the same by the Nominee on behalf of the Foundation. 
2.2 The Nominee shall be restricted to carry out each of the following actions only upon receipt of specific Instructions, delivered in accordance with Clause 7, authorizing and requesting same: 
(a) receive or Deliver any Assets, except as otherwise specifically provided for in this Agreement; 
(b) carry out any other action affecting Assets, other than those specified in Clause 2.3 below, provided, however, that each instance shall be subject to the prior agreement of the Nominee; and 
(c) transfer Assets in connection with this Agreement as the case may be. 
2.3 Absent a contrary Instruction, the Nominee shall be permitted, and is hereby authorised and directed by the Foundation to, and may authorise any other relevant third-party to, carry out any of the following actions without any further Instructions or approval by or on behalf of the Foundation: 
(a) in the Foundation’s name or on its behalf, sign any affidavits, certificates of ownership and other certificates and documents relating to Assets which may be required (i) to obtain any Assets; or (ii) by any tax or regulatory authority having jurisdiction over the Assets; 
(b) notify the Foundation of notices, circulars, reports and announcements that require discretionary action, in each case, which the Nominee has received in the course of acting in its capacity as Nominee under the Agreement; and 
(c) Attend to all non-discretionary matters in connection with anything provided in this Clause 2.3 or any Instruction. 
2.4 Schedule 1 sets forth a summary description of the Nominee’s methods for implementing Clause 2.1, which methods shall ensure that all Assets or indicia of ownership thereof are maintained separately from, and are separately identifiable from, any Digital Assets owned or held by the Nominee for itself or any other person. 
3. DUTIES OF THE NOMINEE 
3.1 The Nominee shall exercise all due skill, care and diligence in the performance of the Services under this Agreement. The Nominee shall owe no other duties to the Foundation other than those duties to perform the Services in accordance with the standard of this Clause 3.1. 
3.2 The Nominee shall only be responsible for the performance of those duties as are expressly set forth herein, including the performance of any Proper Instructions given in accordance with this Agreement. 
4. TAXATION 
4.1 The Foundation shall, for all tax purposes, be treated as the owner of all Assets held by the Nominee pursuant to this Agreement.  It is the Foundation’s sole responsibility to determine whether and to what extent Taxes and Tax reporting obligations may apply to the Foundation with respect to its Assets, and transactions, and the Foundation shall timely pay all such Taxes and shall file all returns, reports, and disclosures required by applicable law.  
4.2 Upon execution of this Agreement, as well as upon request of the Foundation, the Nominee will promptly provide the Foundation with all forms, certifications, documentation, representations and warranties and any other information as the Foundation may request (the Assets Tax Documentation). If any such Assets Tax Documentation becomes inaccurate, incorrect or obsolete or requires supplemental information, the Nominee will notify the Foundation immediately and promptly provide updated Assets Tax Documentation. In the event the Nominee receives any request or audit from any applicable taxing authority or other Governmental Authority with respect to information regarding the Foundation Assets and transactions, the Nominee agrees to immediately inform the Foundation of such request or audit. 
5. NDEMNITY 
5.1 The Foundation agrees to indemnify, defend and hold harmless the Nominee against any and all claims, costs, causes of action, losses, liabilities, lawsuits, demands and damages, fines, penalties and expenses, including without limitation, any and all court costs and reasonable attorney’s fees and any Taxes or other amounts for which the Nominee is liable in any way related to or arising out of or in connection with this Agreement or any action taken or not taken pursuant hereto, except to the extent caused by the Nominee’s gross negligence or willful misconduct. The foregoing indemnifications shall survive any termination of this Agreement. 
5.2 The rights and obligations contained in this Clause 5 shall survive the termination of this Agreement. 
6. TERMINATION 
6.1 The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and terminate when terminated pursuant to this Clause 6. 
6.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement in whole or in part, with or without cause, by giving not less than 30 days’ prior written notice to the other Party. 
6.3 Without prejudice to any accrued rights and remedies under this Agreement, either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately by giving written notice to the other Party upon the occurrence of any of the following events (provided such notice to terminate is given within three months after the occurrence of the event that the other Party commits any material breach of any of its obligations under this Agreement and, in the case of any breach which is capable of remedy, fails to remedy such breach within seven days after delivery of a written notice to the other Party specifying such breach (or such longer period as the notice may specify). 
6.4 Upon termination of this Agreement the Nominee shall deliver the Foundation’s Assets pursuant to Proper Instructions given by the Foundation in writing. If by the termination date the Foundation has not given instructions to the Nominee regarding where to deliver any Assets, the Nominee will continue to safekeep such Assets until the Foundation provides such Proper Instructions to effect a free delivery of such, and the Foundation shall be liable to pay monthly storage fees in the amount determined by the Nominee until all Assets are removed. 
7. ASSIGNMENT AND TRANSFER 
Neither Party may charge, assign or otherwise deal with all or any of its rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior consent of the other Party.

8. NON-WAIVER 
No failure to exercise, nor any delay in exercising, on the part of any Party, any right or remedy under this Agreement shall operate as a waiver of any such right or remedy. No single or partial exercise of any right or remedy shall prevent any further or other exercise or the exercise of any other right or remedy. The rights and remedies provided in this Agreement are cumulative and not exclusive of any rights or remedies provided by Law.

9. MISCELLANEOUS 
9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement and understanding of the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all oral communication and prior writings with respect thereto. 
9.2 Unless otherwise provided for in this Agreement, no amendment in respect of this Agreement will be effective unless executed by the Parties. 
9.3 Except as provided in this Agreement, the rights, powers, remedies and privileges provided in this Agreement are cumulative and not exclusive of any rights, powers, remedies and privileges provided by Law. 
9.4 This Agreement (and each amendment in respect of it) may be executed and delivered in counterparts (including electronically), each of which will be deemed an original. 
9.5 A person who is not a Party to this Agreement has no right under this Agreement to enforce any terms of this Agreement, but this does not affect any right or remedy of a third party which exists under applicable Laws. 
9.6 If any provision of this Agreement is or becomes illegal, invalid, or unenforceable under any applicable Law, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect (as shall that provision under any other Law). 
9.7 As of the Effective Date of this Agreement, and during any period for which the Nominee serves as an authorized agent to the Foundation, the Nominee shall remain, in compliance with each of the Laws, rules and regulations to which the Foundation’s activities are subject and shall notify the Foundation immediately of any notices that are received regarding an investigation of the Foundation’s business or operations; as well as notice of any restrictions or limitations placed on the Foundation’s business by any Governmental Authority. The Nominee further acknowledges that, although the Foundation performs certain activities as part of its own efforts to comply with economic sanctions and trade regulations administered by various authorities, including the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, the Commodities and Futures Trading Commission, the United Nations, the Office for Financial Sanctions Implementation and the U.S. Treasury Department’s Office of Foreign Assets Control, the Foundation’s compliance activities in this regard do not have the effect of the Foundation assuming either (i) responsibility for any of our legal and/or regulatory obligations, or (ii) liability for our failure to comply with any applicable Laws or regulations. 
9.8 The Nominee understands and acknowledges that the Foundation may disclose information about the Nominee, the Nominee’s business and the relationship with the Foundation, including name, address, contact information, the products and services used, and non-personally identifiable information to its affiliates.  The Foundation also may share the aforementioned information about the Nominee with any third party service provider of the Foundation as may be required by applicable Laws or regulations. 
9.9 The Nominee (i) represents and acknowledges that the Nominee had the opportunity to read and understand the terms of this Agreement (including any other related exhibits, attachments or other documentation) and (ii) will comply with and not take any action that could conflict with or could cause a breach of this Agreement (including any related exhibits, attachments or documentation).

This Agreement any non-contractual obligations arising out of or in connection with this Agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Delaware. The Parties irrevocably agree for the benefit of each other that the courts of Delaware shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any disputes which may arise out of or in connection with this Agreement.
